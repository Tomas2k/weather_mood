## 2. Information Collection and Use

To provide weather services, AI character experiences, saved settings, and app functionality, we may collect the following information:

### Location Information

The app may access your device location to provide local weather forecasts and related weather information.

Location access is optional, but some features may not function correctly if permission is denied.

### User Identifiers

We may collect anonymous identifiers, device identifiers, or Firebase-generated identifiers to:

* save app preferences,
* manage unlocked characters,
* store saved cities,
* support anonymous login,
* and maintain app functionality across sessions.

### Device Information

We may collect non-identifiable device information such as:

* device model,
* operating system version,
* app version,
* crash logs,
* and performance data

to improve application stability and user experience.

### Advertising ID

The app may use the Google Advertising ID for advertising, analytics, and AdMob services.

### Purchase Information

If you make in-app purchases, purchase-related information may be processed through Google Play services in order to validate and restore purchased content.

We do not directly collect or store your payment card information.
